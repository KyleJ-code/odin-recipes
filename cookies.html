Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Description

This is the best chocolate chip cookies you will ever eat. They are easy and cheap to make not to mention great for the family

It is chocolatey delicious with a chewy texture. They are just like grandma used to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 sticks butter
  • 2c Flour
  • 1/4c white Sugar
  • 1/2c brown sugar
  • 1tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1c chocolate chips
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheight
  2. mix eggs, both sugars, softened butter
  3. Mix flour, baking powder, and salt
  4. Mix together flour mixture and butter mixture
  5. Combine the chocolate chips and mix thoroughly
  6. Scoop out 1/4c sized balls and put them evenly space on cookie sheet
  7. Bake for 9 minutes
  8. Let rest for a few minutes and enjoy!
