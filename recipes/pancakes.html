Pancake Recipe

Description

This is the best pancakes you will ever eat. This recipe will become a staple in your kitchen.

It is warm, fluffy and tasty. Your family will enjoy this with you.

Ingredients

  • 1c Flour
  • 1/4c Sugar
  • 1/2c milk
  • 1T butter
  • 1tsp salt
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet
  2. wisk eggs together
  3. Mix together flour, sugar, and salt
  4. Mix eggs, milk, and melted butter
  5. Mix together dry and wet ingredients
  6. pour 1/4c batter onto skillet
  7. Cook and flip over after 5 minutes
  8. Serve with butter and syrup
