Apple Pie Recipe

Description

This is the best apple pie you will ever eat. You'll want to make it even on non holiday days

It has warm juicy apples and crispy crust. One bite and you'll feel like you are in an orchard.

Ingredients

  • 5 apples
  • 1c Flour
  • 1/4c Sugar
  • 1/2c water
  • 1tsp cinnammon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheight
  2. Cut up apples
  3. Mix apples with cinnammon and sugar
  4. Mix together flour and water
  5. Flatten out dough into a circle
  6. Bake pie for 20 minutes
  7. Let rest for a few minutes and enjoy!
