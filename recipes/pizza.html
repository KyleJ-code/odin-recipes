Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

This is the best pizza you will ever eat. You'll want to make this pizza every weekend.

It has a nice crispy crust and melty cheese and delicious pepperoni.

Ingredients

  • 1c cheese
  • 1c Self Rising Flour
  • 1/4c water
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/2c pepperoni
  • 1/2c Sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheight
  2. prepare the dough by mixing flour, water, and salt
  3. kneed dough and allow to rise
  4. Flip dough in air and flatten in a pie shape
  5. Flatten out dough into a circle
  6. Add sauce
  7. Add cheese and pepperoni
  8. Bake pizza for 13 minutes
  9. Let rest for a few minutes and enjoy!
