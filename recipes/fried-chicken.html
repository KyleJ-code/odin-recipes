Fried Chicken Recipe

Description

This is the best fried chicken you will ever eat. You're neighbors will want to come over to try your chicken.

It has crispy breading and juicy insides.

Ingredients

  • 1Ib chicken
  • 1/2c Flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4c potatoe starch
  • 1liter oil

Steps

  1. Prepare your chicken
  2. Prepare your dry mixture
  3. Dip chicken in dry mixture
  4. Dip chicken in hot oil for 10 minutes
  5. Remove and let rest on towel
  6. Enjoy!
